# Gothenburg (Goteborg)

Gothenburg is Sweden's second-largest city, located on the west coast. It is known for its maritime heritage, friendly atmosphere, and dynamic industrial and academic environment.

Port city on the west coast. Highlights include automotive industry, Chalmers University, Liseberg theme park, and maritime culture.

## Key Features

- Home to Volvo Group and Volvo Cars
- The Port of Gothenburg: Scandinavia's largest port
- Chalmers University of Technology: a leading engineering institution

## Cultural Attractions

- Universeum science center
- Liseberg amusement park
- Gothenburg Museum of Art
- Haga district with historical wooden houses

## Nature

The Gothenburg archipelago offers scenic views, coastal hiking routes, and boating opportunities.
