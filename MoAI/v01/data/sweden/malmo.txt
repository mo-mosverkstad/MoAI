# Malmo

Malmo is the third-largest city in Sweden, located in the southern region of Skane. It is connected to Copenhagen via the Oresund Bridge, forming a major cross-border metropolitan area.

Southern Swedish hub connected to Copenhagen by Oresund Bridge. Known for diversity, sustainable urban planning, and modern architecture.

## Diversity and Culture

Malmo is known for its multicultural population and vibrant arts scene.

Landmarks:
- Turning Torso skyscraper
- Malmo Castle
- Mollevangen district

## Economy

Strong sectors include:
- Green energy
- ICT
- Logistics
- Creative industries

## Sustainability

Malmo is a global leader in sustainable urban development, with eco-friendly districts such as Vastra Hamnen.
