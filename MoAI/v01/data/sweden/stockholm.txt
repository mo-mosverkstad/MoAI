# Stockholm - Capital of Sweden

Stockholm is Sweden's capital, built across 14 islands where Lake Malaren meets the Baltic Sea. It is known for its clean environment, vibrant cultural life, and modern Nordic architecture.

Capital of Sweden, built on 14 islands. Known for high technology ecosystem, historical center, museums and Nobel events, and a large archipelago.

## History and Culture

Stockholm has been the political and economic center of Sweden for centuries. The Old Town (Gamla Stan) contains medieval streets, the Royal Palace, and historic churches.

Major cultural attractions:
- Vasa Museum
- Skansen Open-Air Museum
- ABBA Museum
- Stockholm City Hall (Nobel Prize banquet)
- Moderna Museet (modern art)

## Technology Hub

Stockholm is a major European tech hub, home to companies such as:
- Spotify
- Klarna
- King (Candy Crush)
- Mojang (Minecraft)

The city supports a thriving startup ecosystem.

## Life and Nature

Stockholm has extensive green areas, nature parks, and surrounding archipelago with over 30,000 islands.
