# Plants

Plants form the base of most food webs through photosynthesis. They convert sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into oxygen and glucose.

Photosynthetic organisms producing oxygen and forming base of food webs.

## Types of Plants

- Flowering plants (angiosperms)
- Conifers (gymnosperms): cone-bearing plants like pine trees
- Ferns: reproduce via spores
- Mosses and liverworts: non-vascular plants
- Algae: simple aquatic plants

## Structures

Typical plant structures include:
- Roots: absorption of water and nutrients, and anchoring
- Stems: transport of water and nutrients, structural support
- Leaves: photosynthesis, gas exchange
- Flowers: reproduction

## Ecological Roles

Plants provide:
- Oxygen production
- CO2 absorption
- Habitat for animals
- Soil stabilization
- Food resources (fruits, seeds, leaves)
