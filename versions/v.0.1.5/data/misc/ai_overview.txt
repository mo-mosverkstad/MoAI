# Artificial Intelligence Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) studies how to create machines capable of performing tasks that require human-like intelligence.

AI aims to build machines able to learn and reason.

## Subfields

- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Natural Language Processing
- Computer Vision
- Reinforcement Learning
- Robotics

## Applications

AI is used in:
- Search engines
- Autonomous vehicles
- Recommendation systems
- Medical diagnostics
- Fraud detection
- Language translation
- Industrial automation

Search engines, self-driving cars, translation, and recommendation systems are among the most prominent AI applications today.

## Ethical and Social Implications

AI raises concerns regarding:
- Privacy
- Bias in training data
- Impact on employment
- Transparency and explainability

Data bias, transparency, privacy, and automation impact are key ethical concerns in the development and deployment of AI systems.
